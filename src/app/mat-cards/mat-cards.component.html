Shiba Inu

The Shiba Inu is the smallest of the six native Dog breeds: Akita, Kishu, Hokkaido, Kai, Shikoku, and Shiba. Shiba is a small, agile dog that copes very well with mountainous terrain, the Shiba Inu is originally bred for hunting.
